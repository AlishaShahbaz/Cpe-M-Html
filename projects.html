Church Building Projects

Our mission is to facilitate the construction of churches that serve as hubs for spirituality, community engagement, and positive change. By undertaking church building projects, we empower local communities with the skills and resources necessary to construct and maintain places of worship that reflect their values and aspirations.

Vocational Bible School (VBS)

CPE-Ministry has a burden to educate and train the Christian Children in Context of Holy Bible. The Church seeks to focus on these major areas which are as below. Physical Growth,Mental Growth,Spiritual Growth,Social Growth,Art Work,Writing,Singing and Speech.

Sewing Center Programs

CPE-Ministry aims to empower poor and marginalized women by reducing poverty, increasing income-generating opportunities, and improving their lifestyle. We are establishing a Women’s Sewing center offering free courses in garment operation and dress making. We are helping women establish and manage small businesses and get employment in local garment factories.

Women's Programs

Our aim is to reach out to widows in our community and support them in adult education, skills training, and monthly grocery. We have provided support for their daughter’s wedding expenses and help with dowry so that they can live with dignity.
